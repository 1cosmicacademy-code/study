## 3. ūüďĚ Grammar: Imperative and Writing a Recipe

### The Imperative

| Person | Rule | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| **du** (informal singular) | Verb stem + (e) (remove -st) | Schneid**e** die Zwiebeln! ‚ÄĒ Cut the onions! |
| **ihr** (informal plural) | Verb stem + t | Schneid**et** die Zwiebeln! ‚ÄĒ Cut the onions! (you all) |
| **Sie** (formal) | Verb stem + en (after Sie) | Schneid**en** Sie die Zwiebeln! ‚ÄĒ Cut the onions! (formal) |
| **wir** (invitation) | Verb stem + en (after wir) | **Lass** uns kochen! ‚ÄĒ Let's cook! |

### Strong Verbs in the Imperative

| Verb | du-Imperative (vowel change) |
|------|------------------------------|
| **essen** ‚ÄĒ to eat | **Iss** more fruit! |
| **geben** ‚ÄĒ to give | **Gib** me the recipe! |
| **lesen** ‚ÄĒ to read | **Lies** the instructions! |
| **nehmen** ‚ÄĒ to take | **Nimm** a spoon! |
| **sehen** ‚ÄĒ to see | **Sieh** dir das an! ‚ÄĒ Look at this! |
| **laufen** ‚ÄĒ to run | **Lauf** faster! |
