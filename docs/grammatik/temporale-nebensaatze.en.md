# Temporal Subordinate Clauses (wenn, als, während, bevor)

[🔙 Back to Grammar Overview](../index.md)

---

## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Use temporal conjunctions to place actions in time
- Distinguish the four key conjunctions **wenn**, **als**, **während**, **bevor**
- Remember the verb position at the end of the subordinate clause (Nebensatz)

---

## 📚 Core Rules

| Conjunction | Typical Meaning | Example with Subordinate Clause |
|------------|----------------|---------------------------------|
| **wenn** | if / whenever (future or repeated) | *Wenn es regnet, bleiben wir zu Hause.* *(If it rains, we stay at home.)*
| **als** | when (single past event) | *Als ich ein Kind war, spielte ich im Park.* *(When I was a child, I played in the park.)*
| **während** | while (simultaneous actions) | *Er liest ein Buch, während sie kocht.* *(He reads a book while she cooks.)*
| **bevor** | before (action that precedes another) | *Bevor ich fahre, trinke ich Kaffee.* *(Before I drive, I drink coffee.)*

> ⚠️ **Note:** The verb in the Nebensatz always goes to the end of that clause.

---

## ✅ Examples

- *Wenn ich nach Berlin komme, besuche ich das Museum.* *(When I come to Berlin, I visit the museum.)*
- *Als er ankam, war die Party bereits im Gange.* *(When he arrived, the party was already in progress.)*
- *Sie hört Musik, während er Hausaufgaben macht.* *(She listens to music while he does homework.)*
- *Bevor wir essen, waschen wir die Hände.* *(Before we eat, we wash our hands.)*
