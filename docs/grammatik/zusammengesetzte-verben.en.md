## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Understand what compound (separable and inseparable) verbs are.
- Learn how the verb stem is positioned in a sentence (verb bracket).
- Distinguish between separable and inseparable prefixes.
- Apply the rule in everyday sentences.

---

## 📚 Core Rule

Compound verbs consist of a **prefix** + **verb stem**.  
- **Separable verbs (trennbare Verben)**: the prefix moves to the end of the clause in main clauses.
- **Inseparable verbs (untrennbare Verben)**: the prefix stays attached to the verb.

### Overview Table

| Verb (with prefix) | Separable? | Meaning |
|---------------------|------------|---------|
| **aufstehen** (steht **auf**) | Yes | to get up |
| **einladen** (lädt **ein**) | Yes | to invite |
| **verstehen** | No | to understand |
| **bekommen** | No | to receive |

## 📝 Example Sentences

- **Ich stehe um 7 Uhr **auf**.** — I get up at 7 o’clock.
- **Wir laden unsere Freunde **ein**.** — We invite our friends.
- **Sie versteht das Problem.** — She understands the problem.
- **Er bekommt ein Geschenk.** — He receives a gift.
