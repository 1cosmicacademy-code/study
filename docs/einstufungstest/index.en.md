> 🎯 **Discover your German level** — A 30-question diagnostic test that estimates your level (A1/A2/B1) and reveals strengths, weaknesses, and personalised recommendations.

## 🎯 Why Take This Test?

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **What is my current level?** | A1 (Beginner) / A2 (Elementary) / B1 (Intermediate) |
| **What are my strengths?** | Areas where you answered correctly |
| **What are my weaknesses?** | Grammar topics and lessons to review |
| **Where should I start?** | A precise recommendation on level and lesson |
| **Which grammar do I need?** | Direct links to each grammar topic |
| **Which exercises to solve?** | Links to exercises matching your level |

## 📋 Instructions

1. **Answer all questions** — Choose the correct answer or write it in
2. **Don't use a dictionary** — This is a diagnostic test to measure your current knowledge
3. **There's no "wrong" answer** — The test only reveals strengths and weaknesses
4. **A full report** with strengths, weaknesses, and recommendations will appear after clicking **Start Assessment**
