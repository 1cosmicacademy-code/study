# 📝 Lektion 3 Exercises: In unserer Freizeit (In Our Free Time)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Talk about hobbies and free time, use separable verbs (Trennbare Verben)
> 💡 **Tip:** In separable verbs, the prefix goes to the end of the sentence when conjugated: **einkaufen** (to shop) → Ich kaufe im Supermarkt **ein**.

## Exercise 2: Separable Verbs (Trennbare Verben) 🔗

**Complete the sentences with the correct separable verb form. Verbs:**
**aufstehen** (to get up), **einkaufen** (to shop), **fernsehen** (to watch TV), **einladen** (to invite), **mitkommen** (to come along)

1. I \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ early tomorrow \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_. (aufstehen)
2. My mother \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ on Saturday \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_. (einkaufen)
3. \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ you \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ tonight? (fernsehen)
4. We \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ our friends to dinner \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_. (einladen)
5. \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ you (pl.) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ to the concert? (mitkommen)

✅ **Solution:**

| Sentence | Answer | Verb + Prefix |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | **get** ... **up** | aufstehen → auf- (up) |
| 2 | **shops** ... no special prefix | einkaufen → ein- (in) |
| 3 | **Do** ... **watch TV** | fernsehen → fern- (far) |
| 4 | **invite** ... (no special prefix needed) | einladen → ein- (in) |
| 5 | **Will** ... **come along** | mitkommen → mit- (with) |

## Exercise 3: Sentence Building ✍️

**Arrange the words to form a correct sentence:**

1. ich / Am Abend / sehe / fern / gern / .
2. du / Wann / auf / morgen / stehst / ?
3. kauft / Sie / im Supermarkt / ein / jeden Samstag / .
4. zum Fußball / kommen / ihr / mit / ?
5. laden / zum Geburtstag / wir / unsere Freunde / ein / .

✅ **Solution:**

| Correct Order |
|---------------|
| 1. Am Abend sehe ich gern fern. (In the evening I like to watch TV.) |
| 2. Wann stehst du morgen auf? (When do you get up tomorrow?) |
| 3. Sie kauft jeden Samstag im Supermarkt ein. (She shops at the supermarket every Saturday.) |
| 4. Kommt ihr zum Fußball mit? (Are you coming along to football?) |
| 5. Wir laden unsere Freunde zum Geburtstag ein. (We invite our friends to the birthday party.) |

## Exercise 4: Dialogue — Free Time 🎯

**Complete the dialogue between Lisa and Tom:**

**Lisa:** Hi Tom, do you have plans for the weekend?

**Tom:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Lisa:** I'd like to go to the cinema. Will you come along?

**Tom:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Lisa:** The movie starts at 7 PM. Is that okay for you?

**Tom:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Lisa:** Great! Should we grab something to eat beforehand?

**Tom:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Lisa:** Good, then let's meet at 5:30 PM at the station.

**Tom:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

✅ **Solution:**

**Lisa:** Hi Tom, do you have plans for the weekend?

**Tom:** No, nothing yet. What would you like to do?

**Lisa:** I'd like to go to the cinema. Will you come along?

**Tom:** Sure! Which movie is playing?

**Lisa:** The movie starts at 7 PM. Is that okay for you?

**Tom:** Yes, perfect. I'll be there at 6:45 PM.

**Lisa:** Great! Should we grab something to eat beforehand?

**Tom:** Good idea! We can eat at the pizzeria around the corner.

**Lisa:** Good, then let's meet at 5:30 PM at the station.

**Tom:** Agreed! See you Saturday!

## Exercise 5: Write About Your Hobbies ✍️

**Write a paragraph (40-60 words) about your hobbies and free time. Use the questions as a guide:**

- What do you like to do in your free time?
- What hobbies do you have?
- Do you play sports? Which ones?
- Do you meet friends?
- Do you like watching TV or do you prefer reading?

✅ **Sample Solution:**

> In my free time I like to do sports. I play tennis and go swimming once a week. I also like reading novels and listening to music. On weekends I often meet up with friends. We cook together or go for a walk. I rarely watch TV.
