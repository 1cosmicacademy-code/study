# 📝 Lektion 1 Exercises: Unser neuer Weg (Our New Path)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Introduce yourself, describe directions, use verb conjugation
> 💡 **Tip:** Pay attention to the difference between **sich vorstellen** (to introduce oneself) and **vorstellen** (to introduce someone else)

---

## Exercise 1: Fill in the Blanks 🖊️

**Complete the sentences with the correct conjugated verbs from the box:**

| stellen | heißen | kommen | wohnen | sein | lernen |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|------|-------|

1. Ich \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ Maria and \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ from Spain.
2. Where \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ you? — I \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ in Berlin.
3. We \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ German for our new path.
4. That \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ my brother. He \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ Thomas.
5. \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ you (pl.) introduce yourselves? — Yes, I \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ myself.

✅ **Solution:**

| Sentence | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | heiße — komme |
| 2 | wohnst — wohne |
| 3 | lernen |
| 4 | ist — heißt |
| 5 | Stellt — stelle |

## Exercise 3: Complete the Dialogue ✍️

**Complete the dialogue between Anna (new employee) and Herr Müller (the manager):**

**Herr Müller:** Good morning! Are you the new colleague?

**Anna:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Herr Müller:** I'm Hans Müller. Nice to meet you!

**Anna:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Herr Müller:** Where are you from, Ms. ...?

**Anna:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Herr Müller:** And where do you live now?

**Anna:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Herr Müller:** Welcome to the team!

**Anna:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

✅ **Solution:**

**Herr Müller:** Good morning! Are you the new colleague?

**Anna:** Yes, exactly. I'm Anna Berger.

**Herr Müller:** I'm Hans Müller. Nice to meet you!

**Anna:** Nice to meet you too, Herr Müller.

**Herr Müller:** Where are you from, Ms. Berger?

**Anna:** I'm from Austria, from Salzburg.

**Herr Müller:** And where do you live now?

**Anna:** I now live in Frankfurt, on Mainzer Straße.

**Herr Müller:** Welcome to the team!

**Anna:** Thank you very much! I'm looking forward to working together.

## Exercise 4: Multiple Choice (Verb Conjugation) 🎯

**Choose the correct verb conjugation:**

1. Ich \_\_\_\_\_ (to be) \_\_\_\_\_ new here.
   - a) bist &emsp; b) **bin** &emsp; c) seid &emsp; d) ist

2. \_\_\_\_\_ (to live) \_\_\_\_\_ you with your family?
   - a) **Wohnst** &emsp; b) Wohne &emsp; c) Wohnt &emsp; d) Wohnen

3. My parents \_\_\_\_\_ (to live) \_\_\_\_\_ in a small village.
   - a) wohne &emsp; b) wohnst &emsp; c) wohnt &emsp; d) **wohnen**

4. \_\_\_\_\_ (to be) \_\_\_\_\_ you (pl.) satisfied with the new path?
   - a) Bin &emsp; b) Bist &emsp; c) **Seid** &emsp; d) Sind

5. Herr Schmidt \_\_\_\_\_ (to come) \_\_\_\_\_ from Switzerland.
   - a) komme &emsp; b) kommst &emsp; c) **kommt** &emsp; d) kommen

✅ **Solution:**

| Question | Correct Answer |
|----------|----------------|
| 1 | b) Ich **bin** neu hier. |
| 2 | a) **Wohnst** du bei deiner Familie? |
| 3 | d) Meine Eltern **wohnen** in einem kleinen Dorf. |
| 4 | c) **Seid** ihr mit dem neuen Weg zufrieden? |
| 5 | c) Herr Schmidt **kommt** aus der Schweiz. |

## Exercise 5: Write About Yourself ✍️

**Write a short paragraph (30-50 words) in German introducing yourself. Use the following information:**

- Your name
- Your country / city
- Where you live
- Your profession (or what you study)
- One hobby

✅ **Sample Solution:**

> Hello! My name is Sarah and I come from Egypt. I live in Cairo in a small apartment. By profession I am an engineer. In my free time I like to read books. This is my new path in Germany.
