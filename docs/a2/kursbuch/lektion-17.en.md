## 3. 📝 Grammar: Adjective Declension (Nominative, Accusative, Dative)

### Adjective Declension — Nominative

| Gender | Definite Article | Indefinite Article |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **masculine** (der) | der **alte** Mann | ein **alter** Mann |
| **feminine** (die) | die **alte** Frau | eine **alte** Frau |
| **neuter** (das) | das **alte** Haus | ein **altes** Haus |
| **Plural** (die) | die **alten** Männer | — |

### Adjective Declension — Accusative

| Gender | Definite Article | Indefinite Article |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **masculine** (den) | den **alten** Mann | einen **alten** Mann |
| **feminine** (die) | die **alte** Frau | eine **alte** Frau |
| **neuter** (das) | das **alte** Haus | ein **altes** Haus |
| **Plural** (die) | die **alten** Männer | — |

### Adjective Declension — Dative

| Gender | Definite Article | Indefinite Article |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **masculine** (dem) | dem **alten** Mann | einem **alten** Mann |
| **feminine** (der) | der **alten** Frau | einer **alten** Frau |
| **neuter** (dem) | dem **alten** Haus | einem **alten** Haus |
| **Plural** (den) | den **alten** Männern | — |

> ⚠️ **Important Notes:**
> - In **Nominative / Accusative**: adjectives take -e (with der/die/das) or -en (in Dative, Genitive, and Plural)
> - In **Dative**: always **-en** regardless of gender!
> - With **indefinite article**: the adjective takes the gender ending (e.g., -er for masculine, -es for neuter)

### Examples

| Deutsch | English |
|---------|---------|
| Der **neue** Laden ist toll. | The **new** store is great. |
| Ich kaufe einen **billigen** Tisch. | I'm buying a **cheap** table. |
| Mit dem **alten** Auto fahre ich nicht. | I don't drive the **old** car. |
| Ich suche ein **praktisches** Geschenk. | I'm looking for a **practical** gift. |
| Die **roten** Schuhe gefallen mir. | I like the **red** shoes. |
| Sie wohnt in einer **kleinen** Wohnung. | She lives in a **small** apartment. |

## 4. 💬 Dialogue: Shopping

**Saleswoman:** Good day! Can I help you?

**Customer:** Yes, I'm looking for a warm sweater.

**Saleswoman:** Which colour would you like?

**Customer:** A blue sweater, please. In size L.

**Saleswoman:** Here is a nice, blue sweater. It's made of wool and very comfortable.

**Customer:** May I try it on?

**Saleswoman:** Of course! The fitting room is over there on the left.

**Customer:** (after trying it on) I like the sweater. How much does it cost?

**Saleswoman:** It costs 39.99 euros. It's on special offer.

**Customer:** Great! I'll take it. Can I pay by card?

**Saleswoman:** Yes, of course. That's 39.99 euros.

**Customer:** Here's my card. Thank you very much!
