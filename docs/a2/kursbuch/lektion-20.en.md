## 3. 📝 Grammar: Subordinate Clauses with "als" (Past)

### "als" for One-Time Events in the Past

> ⚠️ **Note:** We use **als** for single/one-time events in the past (NOT **wenn**). Use **wenn** for repeated events or in the present/future tense.

### The Rule

| Main Clause | Subordinate Clause with "als" |
|-------------|------------------------------|
| I was happy | **als** ich ein Kind **war** — when I was a child |
| We drove to the sea | **als** ich zehn Jahre alt **war** — when I was ten |
| She cried | **als** der Film **endete** — when the film ended |
| He called me | **als** er zu Hause **ankam** — when he arrived home |

### Verb Position

> In subordinate clauses with **als**, the verb goes to the **end of the clause**!

| Example | Structure |
|---------|-----------|
| **Als** ich klein **war**, spielte ich gern draußen. | als + subject + ... + verb at the end |
| I felt free **als** ich im Urlaub **war**. | Main clause + als + subject + ... + verb at the end |

### Comparison: als vs. wenn

| als (Past — one time) | wenn (Repeated or Present/Future) |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------|
| **Als** I **was** a child, I had a dog. (single continuous phase) | **Wenn** you **are** sick, stay home. (if — repeated) |
| **Als** I **turned** 18, I got a car. (specific moment) | **Wenn** I **have** time, I like to read. (habit) |
| **Als** we **were** in Paris, we visited the Eiffel Tower. (one specific trip) | **Wenn** we travel, we are always happy. (repeated) |

### More Examples

| Deutsch | English |
|---------|---------|
| **Als** ich klein **war**, hatte ich Angst im Dunkeln. | When I was little, I was afraid of the dark. |
| **Als** der Unterricht **endete**, gingen wir nach Hause. | When the lesson ended, we went home. |
| **Als** ich das erste Mal flog, war ich sehr aufgeregt. | When I flew for the first time, I was very excited. |
| **Als** er die Wahrheit **erfuhr**, war er traurig. | When he learned the truth, he was sad. |
| **Als** wir uns **kennenlernten**, waren wir 20. | When we met, we were 20. |

## 4. 💬 Dialogue: Sharing Memories

**Anna:** Do you remember when we were children?

**Ben:** Yes, I remember fondly. We always played in the park.

**Anna:** True! When I was little, I often went for walks with my grandpa.

**Ben:** I remember our bike tours. Those were great experiences.

**Anna:** Yes, and when we were 15, we went on our first trip — without parents!

**Ben:** That was exciting! I wish I could be that free again.

**Anna:** What do you wish for the future?

**Ben:** I wish for a trip around the world. And you?

**Anna:** I dream of my own house with a big garden.

**Ben:** That sounds lovely! I hope our wishes come true.

**Anna:** I hope so too!
