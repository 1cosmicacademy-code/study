## 🎯 What Each Lesson Contains

Each lesson in **Treffpunkt Kursbuch A2** contains:

- 🗣️ **Dialogue (Dialog)** — a conversation between two people
- 📝 **New Vocabulary (Wortschatz)** — words with translation and pronunciation
- 📖 **Grammar (Grammatik)** — simple grammar explanations with tables
- 💬 **Interactive Talk (Gespräch)** — practical application
- ✏️ **Exercises (Übungen)** — practice what you've learned

> 💡 **Tip:** Study the lessons in order. Each one builds on the previous!
