# Verbs with Prepositions — Verben mit Präpositionen

## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Recognise which German verbs require a specific preposition.
- Understand the meaning of the prepositional phrase that follows each verb.
- Use these verb‑preposition combinations in everyday sentences.

---

## 📚 Core Rule

A verb may be tightly linked with a preposition that determines the case and the nuance of the meaning.  The preposition is part of the verb phrase and cannot be omitted or replaced.

| Verb | Preposition | Meaning (English) |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| **denken** | **an** | think of / about |
| **sich freuen** | **auf** | look forward to |
| **warten** | **auf** | wait for |
| **sprechen** | **mit** | talk with |
| **sich beschäftigen** | **mit** | be occupied with |
| **fahren** | **mit** | travel by |
| **interessieren** | **für** | be interested in |
| **lösen** | **für** | solve for |
| **beschweren** | **über** | complain about |
| **sich ärgern** | **über** | get angry about |

### Example Sentences

- **Ich denke **an** dich.** — I think of you.
- **Wir freuen uns **auf** das Wochenende.** — We look forward to the weekend.
- **Er wartet **auf** den Bus.** — He waits for the bus.
- **Sie spricht **mit** der Lehrerin.** — She talks with the teacher.
- **Ich interessiere mich **für** Kunst.** — I am interested in art.
