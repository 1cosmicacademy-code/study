# 📝 Lektion 4 Exercises: Wohnen und Nachbarn (Living and Neighbors)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Describe housing and neighbors, use **wenn** sentences and local prepositions
> 💡 **Tip:** **wenn** means "when / if" and puts the verb at the end of the clause: **Wenn ich Zeit habe, gehe ich spazieren.** (If I have time, I go for a walk.)

## Exercise 2: Wenn Sentences 🔗

**Connect the two sentences using "wenn":**

1. I have time. I go for a walk.
   → \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

2. My neighbor is loud. I can't sleep.
   → \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

3. It rains. We stay at home.
   → \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

4. I have vacation. I like to travel.
   → \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

5. One lives in the center. It is more expensive.
   → \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

✅ **Solution:**

| Connected Sentence |
|--------------------|
| 1. **Wenn** ich Zeit habe, gehe ich spazieren. |
| 2. **Wenn** meine Nachbarin laut ist, kann ich nicht schlafen. |
| 3. **Wenn** es regnet, bleiben wir zu Hause. |
| 4. **Wenn** ich Urlaub habe, reise ich gern. |
| 5. **Wenn** man im Zentrum wohnt, ist es teurer. |

**Note:** In **wenn** sentences, the verb goes to the end of the conditional clause.

## Exercise 3: Local Prepositions ✍️

**Choose the correct preposition (in, auf, unter, neben, zwischen, vor, hinter):**

1. The lamp is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the table. (on)
2. The book is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the shelf. (in/on)
3. The dog sleeps \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the bed. (under)
4. The desk is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the window. (in front of)
5. The bathroom is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the bedroom. (next to)
6. The garage is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the house. (behind)
7. The sofa is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ the table and the cabinet. (between)

✅ **Solution:**

| Sentence | Preposition |
|----------|-------------|
| 1 | **auf** (The lamp is on the table.) |
| 2 | **in** (The book is in the shelf.) |
| 3 | **unter** (The dog sleeps under the bed.) |
| 4 | **vor** (The desk is in front of the window.) |
| 5 | **neben** (The bathroom is next to the bedroom.) |
| 6 | **hinter** (The garage is behind the house.) |
| 7 | **zwischen** (between the table and the cabinet) |

## Exercise 4: Dialogue — Neighbors 🎯

**Complete the dialogue between Anna (new tenant) and Frau Klein (her neighbor):**

**Anna:** Hello, I'm Anna. I've been living in the apartment next door since today.

**Frau Klein:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Anna:** Yes, I just moved in. It's still a bit chaotic.

**Frau Klein:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Anna:** That's very kind! If I need help, I'll let you know.

**Frau Klein:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Anna:** Quiet time starts at 10 PM, right? That's good to know.

**Frau Klein:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Anna:** Thanks for the information! See you soon!

**Frau Klein:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

✅ **Solution:**

**Anna:** Hello, I'm Anna. I've been living in the apartment next door since today.

**Frau Klein:** Oh, welcome! I'm Mrs. Klein and I've lived here for ten years.

**Anna:** Yes, I just moved in. It's still a bit chaotic.

**Frau Klein:** No problem. If you need help carrying things, let me know.

**Anna:** That's very kind! If I need help, I'll let you know.

**Frau Klein:** Everyone in this building is very friendly. We help each other out.

**Anna:** Quiet time starts at 10 PM, right? That's good to know.

**Frau Klein:** Yes, exactly. But if you ever want to throw a party, just let us know in advance.

**Anna:** Thanks for the information! See you soon!

**Frau Klein:** See you soon, Ms. Anna! And welcome to the building!

## Exercise 5: Describe Your Apartment ✍️

**Write a paragraph (50-70 words) describing your apartment or house. Use the questions as a guide:**

- Do you live in an apartment or a house?
- How many rooms are there?
- What does your apartment have? (balcony, garden, ...)
- What is the location like? (center, suburb, quiet, ...)
- What are your neighbors like?

✅ **Sample Solution:**

> I live in a small apartment in the center. The apartment has three rooms: a living room, a bedroom, and a kitchen. There is also a small balcony. The neighbors are friendly and helpful. When I need help, I just knock on the door. Unfortunately, the rent is a bit high, but the location is perfect.
