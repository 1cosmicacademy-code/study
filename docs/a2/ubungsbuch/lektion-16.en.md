# 📝 Lektion 16 Exercises: Sport und Ernährung (Sports and Nutrition)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Learn sports and nutrition vocabulary, use the imperative form (Imperativ), write recipes and health instructions.
>
> 💡 **Tip:** Memorize sports verbs with their prepositions — for example, "sich anmelden zu" and "teilnehmen an" require specific prepositions.

## Exercise 1: Sports Vocabulary — Match the Word 🖊️

**Match the sport with the correct sentence:**

| Sport | Sentence |
|-------|----------|
| 1. Fußball | a. You ride a board on water. |
| 2. Schwimmen | b. You ski on snow with two boards. |
| 3. Skifahren | c. You meet in a club and score goals. |
| 4. Surfen | d. You move in the water with arms and legs. |
| 5. Joggen | e. You lift weights at the gym. |
| 6. Krafttraining | f. You run in the park or forest. |

✅ **Solution:**
| Sport | Sentence |
|-------|----------|
| 1. Fußball → **c** | You meet in a club and score goals. |
| 2. Schwimmen → **d** | You move in the water with arms and legs. |
| 3. Skifahren → **b** | You ski on snow with two boards. |
| 4. Surfen → **a** | You ride a board on water. |
| 5. Joggen → **f** | You run in the park or forest. |
| 6. Krafttraining → **e** | You lift weights at the gym. |

## Exercise 3: Writing a Recipe with Imperativ ✍️

**Write the following recipe using the imperative form (Imperativ) for "Sie" and for "du":**

*Ingredients: 200g noodles, 2 eggs, salt, pepper, cheese*

| Step | du-form | Sie-form |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1. Boil water | **Koche** das Wasser! | **Kochen** Sie das Wasser! |
| 2. Add noodles | | |
| 3. Cook 10 minutes | | |
| 4. Put eggs in the pan | | |
| 5. Season with salt and pepper | | |
| 6. Sprinkle cheese on top | | |

✅ **Solution:**

| Step | du-form | Sie-form |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1. Boil water | **Koche** das Wasser! | **Kochen** Sie das Wasser! |
| 2. Add noodles | **Füge** die Nudeln hinzu! | **Fügen** Sie die Nudeln hinzu! |
| 3. Cook 10 minutes | **Koche** 10 Minuten! | **Kochen** Sie 10 Minuten! |
| 4. Put eggs in the pan | **Gib** die Eier in die Pfanne! | **Geben** Sie die Eier in die Pfanne! |
| 5. Season with salt and pepper | **Würze** mit Salz und Pfeffer! | **Würzen** Sie mit Salz und Pfeffer! |
| 6. Sprinkle cheese on top | **Streue** den Käse darüber! | **Streuen** Sie den Käse darüber! |

## Exercise 4: Healthy Eating Dialogue 💬

**Complete the dialogue between a doctor and a patient:**

**Arzt:** Guten Tag, Herr Müller. Was __________________ Ihnen?
**Patient:** Guten Tag, Herr Doktor. Ich habe oft __________________ und fühle mich müde.
**Arzt:** __________________ Sie genug?
**Patient:** Ich __________________ nicht so viel. Ich habe wenig Zeit.
**Arzt:** __________________ Sie mehr Obst und Gemüse! Und __________________ Sie regelmäßig Sport!

✅ **Solution:**

**Arzt:** Guten Tag, Herr Müller. Was **fehlt** Ihnen?
**Patient:** Guten Tag, Herr Doktor. Ich habe oft **Kopfschmerzen** und fühle mich müde.
**Arzt:** **Schlafen** Sie genug?
**Patient:** Ich **schlafe** nicht so viel. Ich habe wenig Zeit.
**Arzt:** **Essen** Sie mehr Obst und Gemüse! Und **machen** Sie regelmäßig Sport!

**Complete dialogue:**
**Doctor:** Good morning, Mr. Müller. What's wrong with you?
**Patient:** Good morning, doctor. I often have headaches and feel tired.
**Doctor:** Do you sleep enough?
**Patient:** I don't sleep much. I have little time.
**Doctor:** Eat more fruits and vegetables! And exercise regularly!

## Exercise 5: Write About Your Favorite Sport and Food 📝

**Answer the following questions in complete sentences (3-5 sentences each):**

1. **Welche Sportart machst du gern?** (Which sport do you like to do?)
2. **Wie oft machst du Sport?** (How often do you do sports?)
3. **Was isst du am liebsten?** (What's your favorite food?)
4. **Was ist dein Lieblingsgetränk?** (What's your favorite drink?)

✅ **Sample Answer:**

1. **Welche Sportart machst du gern?**
   I like to play soccer. I've been playing in a club for five years. Soccer is fun for me because I can move around a lot.

2. **Wie oft machst du Sport?**
   I do sports three times a week. On Monday, Wednesday, and Friday I go to the gym. On weekends I sometimes play tennis with friends.

3. **Was isst du am liebsten?**
   My favorite food is pizza with cheese and tomatoes. But I also like healthy food like salad and fruit. I try to eat an apple every day.

4. **Was ist dein Lieblingsgetränk?**
   My favorite drink is water with lemon. I also like tea, especially in the evening. I don't like cola and lemonade that much because they have too much sugar.

---

> 💡 **Extra Tip:** When using Imperativ with "du":
> - Regular verbs: **kochen → koch + e** = Koche!
> - Irregular verbs (e → i): **sprechen → sprich!**, **geben → gib!** (no -e ending)
> - Separable verbs: **aufstehen → steh auf!** (the prefix goes to the end)
