## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Form superlative adjectives and adverbs
- Learn the regular **-ste** and irregular **-sten** patterns
- Avoid common pitfalls when using the superlative

---

## 📚 Core Rule

1. **Short adjectives** (usually ending in a consonant) add **-ste** after the adjective:
   - *fast* → *am **fast**sten* (**the fastest**)
2. If the adjective ends in **-e**, **-el**, **-er**, or a vowel‑heavy ending, drop the ending and add **-sten**:
   - *groß* → *am **größ**ten* (**the biggest**)
   - *teuer* → *am **teuer**sten* (**the most expensive**)
3. For adverbs the superlative is built with **am … -sten** as well:
   - *gut* → *am **gut**sten* (**the best**)

> ⚠️ **Common Error:** Adding an extra *e* (e.g., *am schnellest*) is wrong. The correct form is *am **schnell**sten*.

| Adjective | Comparative | Superlative |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| klein | kleiner | am **klein**sten |
| laut | lauter | am **laut**sten |
| teuer | teurer | am **teuer**sten |

---

## ✅ Examples

- **Das schnellste Auto gewinnt das Rennen.** *(The fastest car wins the race.)*
- **Er ist am freundlichsten von allen.** *(He is the friendliest of all.)*
- **Das ist am interessantesten.** *(That is the most interesting.)*
