## Exercise 3: Choose — **nicht** or **kein/keine**? 🎯

**Fill in the blank with **nicht** or **kein/keine**:**

| # | Sentence |
|---|----------|
| 1 | Ich esse ________ Fleisch. (I eat no meat — negating the noun) |
| 2 | Der Kaffee ist ________ heiß. (The coffee is not hot — negating the adjective) |
| 3 | Er trinkt ________ Bier. (He drinks no beer — negating the noun) |
| 4 | Ich habe ________ Hunger. (I am not hungry — negating the noun) |
| 5 | Anna kommt ________. (Anna is not coming — negating the verb) |
| 6 | Das Brot ist ________ frisch. (The bread is not fresh — negating the adjective) |
| 7 | Wir haben ________ Milch. (We have no milk — negating the noun) |
| 8 | Ich mag ________ Fisch. (I don't like fish — negating the noun) |
| 9 | Sie arbeitet ________. (She doesn't work — negating the verb) |
| 10 | Ich trinke ________ Kaffee am Abend. (I drink no coffee in the evening — negating noun) |

</div>

## Exercise 8: Restaurant Dialogue 💬
