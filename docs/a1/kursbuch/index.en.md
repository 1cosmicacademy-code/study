## 🎯 What Each Lesson Contains

Every lesson in **Level A1** includes:

- 🗣️ **Dialogue (Dialog)** — A conversation between two people about the lesson topic
- 📝 **Vocabulary (Wortschatz)** — New words with translations
- 📖 **Grammar (Grammatik)** — Simple grammar explanations
- 🎧 **Listening (Hören)** — Listening comprehension exercises
- ✏️ **Exercises (Übungen)** — Practice what you've learned

> 💡 **Tip:** Study each lesson in order. They build on each other!
