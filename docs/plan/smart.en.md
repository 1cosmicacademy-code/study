# 🚀 Smart Track — Balanced and Sustainable

> **Pace:** 2 lessons/week | **Study days:** 4 days/week | **Hours:** 8 hours/week
>
> **A1: 12 weeks ← A2: 12 weeks ← B1: 8 weeks ← Total: ~8 months**

The recommended track for most learners. It combines consistency with flexibility, making it suitable for those with work or study commitments while maintaining a steady learning momentum.

---

## 🎯 Who Is This Track For?

| Category | Fit |
|:---------|:----:|
| 👨‍💼 **Full-time employee** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 🎓 **University student** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Family & home commitments** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 🏃 **Beginner in German** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| 🗣️ **Wants a comfortable, steady pace** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

This track is ideal if you have **4 days per week** where you can dedicate 2 hours to studying, and you want guaranteed results within a reasonable timeframe without pressure.

---

## ✅ Pros

- 🕐 **High flexibility** — Only 4 days, leaving 3 days for rest and other commitments
- 🧠 **Better retention** — A day between each lesson session lets your brain process information
- 📈 **High completion rate** — Moderate pace prevents burnout
- 💰 **Equivalent to Goethe-Institut courses** — Same duration (~8 months) at no cost
- 🔄 **Optimal scheduling** — New lesson → exercises + grammar → new lesson → exercises + grammar
- 🎯 **Regular review** — Review every 5 lessons (3 times per level)

## ❌ Cons

- 🐢 **Slowest track** — Takes ~8 months to reach B1
- 📅 **Requires self-discipline** — No teacher to follow up with you
- 🔁 **Risk of long breaks** — Stopping for more than a week may affect momentum

## 📅 Weekly Schedule

### Weekly Rhythm Overview

| Day | Duration | Task |
|:---:|:--------:|------|
| 📅 Monday | 2 hours | 📕 **New Kursbuch lesson** + vocabulary |
| 📅 Tuesday | 2 hours | 📝 **Übungsbuch exercises** + 📘 related grammar |
| 📅 Wednesday | 2 hours | 📕 **New Kursbuch lesson** + vocabulary |
| 📅 Thursday | 2 hours | 📝 **Übungsbuch exercises** + 📘 related grammar |
| 🎯 Fri–Sun | — | Rest or **light vocabulary review** |

### Time Breakdown per Day

**Lesson Day (Monday/Wednesday):**
```
📕 2 hours = 120 minutes
 ├── 🎬 Watch lesson video             →  10 min
 ├── 📖 Read explanations & tables     →  40 min
 ├── 💬 Read dialogue & understand     →  15 min
 ├── 📝 Helper sentences & pronunciation → 10 min
 └── 🗂️ Learn new vocabulary           →  25 min
```

**Exercise & Grammar Day (Tuesday/Thursday):**
```
📝📘 2 hours = 120 minutes
 ├── 📝 Interactive Übungsbuch exercises →  30 min
 ├── 🔍 Review solutions & mistakes      →  15 min
 ├── 📘 Read grammar page                →  20 min
 ├── ✍️ Interactive grammar exercises    →  20 min
 └── 🔄 Review previous lesson vocabulary → 15 min
```

## 💡 Smart Track Tips

1. **🎬 Start each week with the video** — Watch Monday's and Wednesday's videos before reading the explanations
2. **📱 Digital flashcards** — Spend 10 minutes daily reviewing new vocabulary
3. **🗓️ Stick to your days** — Monday/Wednesday for lessons, Tuesday/Thursday for exercises
4. **🎯 Don't skip review weeks** — Review weeks (every 5 lessons) are for consolidation, not vacation
5. **💪 Keep going even if you miss a week** — Don't try to cram 2 lessons in one day, just resume where you left off
6. **🗣️ Speak from day one** — Repeat sentences out loud even when alone
