## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Identify the three main negation words used at A2 level.
- Understand how to place them correctly in a sentence.
- Recognize the difference between negating a person, a thing, or an action.
- Use them in everyday contexts.

---

## 📚 Core Rule

| Word | English translation |
|------|---------------------|
| **niemand** | nobody / no one |
| **nichts** | nothing |
| **nie** | never |

### Example Sentences

- **Niemand** hat das Buch gelesen. — Nobody has read the book.
- **Nichts** ist hier. — Nothing is here.
- **Ich habe nie** Deutsch gesprochen. — I have never spoken German.
