## 3. 📝 Grammar: Perfekt Tense (Travel)

### Forming the Perfect Tense

**haben + Partizip II** or **sein + Partizip II**

| Verb | Perfekt | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **reisen** (sein) | **ist gereist** | He **travelled** to Berlin. |
| **fahren** (sein) | **ist gefahren** | We **drove** to the sea. |
| **fliegen** (sein) | **ist geflogen** | She **flew** to Turkey. |
| **bleiben** (sein) | **ist geblieben** | I **stayed** for a week. |
| **ankommen** (sein) | **ist angekommen** | The train **arrived** on time. |
| **buchen** (haben) | **hat gebucht** | He **booked** a hotel. |
| **packen** (haben) | **hat gepackt** | I **packed** my suitcase. |
| **bezahlen** (haben) | **hat bezahlt** | We **paid** for the trip. |
| **sehen** (haben) | **hat gesehen** | I **saw** many sights. |
| **erleben** (haben) | **hat erlebt** | She **experienced** great things. |

### Movement Verbs Take "sein"

> **sein** + Perfekt = verbs of movement and state change
>
> Examples: fahren, fliegen, reisen, kommen, gehen, laufen, schwimmen, wandern, ankommen, abfahren

> **haben** + Perfekt = all other verbs
>
> Examples: buchen, packen, bezahlen, sehen, essen, trinken, fotografieren

### Typical Travel Verbs in Perfekt

| Present | Perfekt | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| ich fahre ans Meer | ich **bin** ans Meer **gefahren** | I went to the sea |
| wir buchen ein Hotel | wir **haben** ein Hotel **gebucht** | we booked a hotel |
| das Flugzeug landet | das Flugzeug **ist gelandet** | the plane landed |
| sie besichtigt die Altstadt | sie **hat** die Altstadt **besichtigt** | she visited the old town |
| wir machen einen Ausflug | wir **haben** einen Ausflug **gemacht** | we went on an excursion |

> ⚠️ **Attention:** The difference between **ist gefahren** and **hat gefahren**:
> - **ist gefahren** = travelled/went (by vehicle) — the subject moved themselves
> - **hat gefahren** = drove (someone/something else) — rare usage

## 4. 💬 Dialogue: Vacation Experiences

**Lisa:** Hi Mark! You look well-rested. Were you on vacation?

**Mark:** Yes, I went to Italy for a week.

**Lisa:** How great! Did you fly or drive?

**Mark:** I travelled by train. The journey was very nice.

**Lisa:** And what did you do?

**Mark:** I visited Rome, took many photos, and ate Italian food.

**Lisa:** Did you book a hotel?

**Mark:** No, I stayed at a youth hostel. It was cheaper.

**Lisa:** How long did you stay?

**Mark:** A week. I came back last week.

**Lisa:** That sounds like a great vacation!

**Mark:** Yes, I brought back wonderful memories!
