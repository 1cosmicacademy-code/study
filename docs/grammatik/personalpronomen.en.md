# Personal Pronouns (Personalpronomen) in German

🎯 **Personal pronouns** replace nouns — instead of repeating the name, you use a pronoun: I, you, he, she, it...

---

## 💡 Pronouns Table (Nominative)

| Person | Pronoun | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| 1st sing | **ich** | I | Ich bin hier. |
| 2nd sing | **du** | you (informal) | Du bist nett. |
| 3rd masc | **er** | he | Er kommt. |
| 3rd fem | **sie** | she | Sie singt. |
| 3rd neut | **es** | it | Es regnet. |
| 1st pl | **wir** | we | Wir lernen. |
| 2nd pl | **ihr** | you all (informal) | Ihr seid lustig. |
| 3rd pl | **sie** | they | Sie tanzen. |
| Formal (s/pl) | **Sie** | you (formal) | Sie sind Herr Müller. |

> **Note:** The formal **Sie** is always capitalized and works for both singular and plural.

## ⚠️ Important Usage

### Difference between du / ihr / Sie

| Pronoun | When to use | Example |
|---------|------------|---------|
| **du** | One person you know well | **Du** bist mein Freund. |
| **ihr** | A group you know well | **Ihr** seid meine Freunde. |
| **Sie** | Formal (strangers, work, elders) | **Sie** sind Herr Schmidt. |

### Special uses of "es"

1. **As personal pronoun:** Das Kind weint. **Es** ist traurig.
2. **Weather:** **Es** regnet. (It's raining.)
3. **Time:** **Es** ist 5 Uhr. (It's 5 o'clock.)
4. **Dummy subject:** **Es** gibt ein Problem. (There is a problem.)

## 📌 Golden Rule

```
ich → I (speaker)
du → you (informal, singular)
er/sie/es → he/she/it
wir → we
ihr → you (informal, plural)
sie → they
Sie → you (formal — capital S!)
```

## ✅ Examples with Translation

| Sentence | Translation |
|----------|------------|
| **Ich** heiße Ali. | My name is Ali. |
| **Du** bist fleißig. | You are hardworking. |
| **Er** ist mein Bruder. | He is my brother. |
| **Sie** ist meine Schwester. | She is my sister. |
| **Es** ist klein. | It is small. |
| **Wir** kommen aus Ägypten. | We come from Egypt. |
| **Ihr** habt ein Haus. | You have a house. |
| **Sie** wohnen in Berlin. | They live in Berlin. |
| **Sie** (formal) sind Herr Doktor. | You are the doctor. |
