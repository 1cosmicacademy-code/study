# 📝 Lektion 20 Exercises: Erinnerungen und Wünsche (Memories and Wishes)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Learn memories and wishes vocabulary, use "als" subordinate clauses for the past, and express wishes using würde + Infinitive.
>
> 💡 **Tip:**
> - **als** = a one-time event in the past (when I was little...)
> - **wenn** = a recurring event in the past (whenever I was at school...)
> - **würde + Infinitive** = a wish/desire (I wish I would...)

---

## Exercise 1: Memory and Wish Vocabulary — Fill in the Blank 🖊️

**Choose the right word for each sentence:**

| die Erinnerung (memory) | der Wunsch (wish) | das Ziel (goal) | die Hoffnung (hope) | die Kindheit (childhood) | der Traum (dream) |
|-------------------------|-------------------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------------------|-------------------|

1. Back in my __________________, I played a lot with my friends.
2. My biggest __________________ is a trip around the world.
3. I have a nice __________________ of my first day of school.
4. My __________________ for next year is to learn German.
5. The __________________ that everything will be okay gives me strength.
6. His __________________ is to have his own house one day.

✅ **Solution:**

1. Back in my **Kindheit** (childhood), I played a lot with my friends.
2. My biggest **Traum** (dream) is a trip around the world.
3. I have a nice **Erinnerung** (memory) of my first day of school.
4. My **Ziel** (goal) for next year is to learn German.
5. The **Hoffnung** (hope) that everything will be okay gives me strength.
6. His **Wunsch** (wish) is to have his own house one day.

## Exercise 2: Subordinate Clauses with "als" 🔗

**Connect the sentences using "als":**

1. I was small. I played a lot with my grandpa.
   → Als ich ____________, ____________ ich viel mit meinem Opa ____________.

2. We were on vacation. It rained every day.
   → Als wir ____________, ____________ es jeden Tag ____________.

3. I came home. My mother was cooking dinner.
   → Als ich ____________, ____________ meine Mutter Abendessen.

4. She heard the news. She was very happy.
   → Als sie ____________, ____________ sie sich sehr.

5. We met for the first time. We were both very nervous.
   → Als wir ____________, ____________ wir beide sehr nervös.

✅ **Solution:**

1. Als ich **klein war**, **spielte** ich viel mit meinem Opa.
   *(When I was little, I played a lot with my grandpa.)*

2. Als wir **im Urlaub waren**, **regnete** es jeden Tag.
   *(When we were on vacation, it rained every day.)*

3. Als ich **nach Hause kam**, **kochte** meine Mutter Abendessen.
   *(When I came home, my mother was cooking dinner.)*

4. Als sie **die Nachricht hörte**, **freute** sie sich sehr.
   *(When she heard the news, she was very happy.)*

5. Als wir **uns zum ersten Mal trafen**, **waren** wir beide sehr nervös.
   *(When we met for the first time, we were both very nervous.)*

**The Rule:**
> **als + Subject + Verb (at the end of the subordinate clause) + Comma + Main clause**
> The verb in the subordinate clause goes to the **end of the clause**!
> "als" is used for a **one-time event in the past**.

## Exercise 3: Expressing Wishes (würde + Infinitive) ✍️

**Transform the sentences into wishes using würde + Infinitive:**

| Original sentence | Wish (würde) |
|------------------|--------------|
| 1. I have a car. | I __________________ a car __________________. |
| 2. She lives in Berlin. | She __________________ in Berlin __________________. |
| 3. We take a world trip. | We __________________ a world trip __________________. |
| 4. He speaks perfect German. | He __________________ perfect German __________________. |
| 5. You (plural) have more time. | You __________________ more time __________________. |
| 6. I can fly. | I __________________ __________________ __________________. |

✅ **Solution:**

| Original sentence | Wish (würde + Infinitiv) |
|------------------|--------------------------|
| 1. I have a car. | I **würde** gern ein Auto **haben**. |
| 2. She lives in Berlin. | She **würde** gern in Berlin **wohnen**. |
| 3. We take a world trip. | Wir **würden** gern eine Weltreise **machen**. |
| 4. He speaks perfect German. | Er **würde** gern perfekt Deutsch **sprechen**. |
| 5. You (plural) have more time. | Ihr **würdet** gern mehr Zeit **haben**. |
| 6. I can fly. | Ich **würde** gern **fliegen können**. |

**Conjugation of würde:**
| Pronoun | würden |
|---------|--------|
| ich | würde |
| du | würdest |
| er/sie/es | würde |
| wir | würden |
| ihr | würdet |
| sie/Sie | würden |

## Exercise 4: Sharing Memories Dialogue 💬

**Complete the dialogue between two friends talking about memories and wishes:**

**Tobias:** Weißt du noch, als wir __________________ (sein) Kinder __________________ (sein)?
**Mia:** Ja, wir __________________ (spielen) jeden Tag zusammen im Park.
**Tobias:** __________________ (erinnern) du dich an unseren Lehrer Herrn Schmidt?
**Mia:** Ja, er __________________ (sein) sehr nett, aber auch streng.
**Tobias:** Was __________________ (machen) du heute gern, wenn du frei __________________ (haben)?
**Mia:** Ich __________________ (würde) gern ans Meer __________________ (fahren).

✅ **Solution:**

**Tobias:** Weißt du noch, als wir **Kinder waren**?
**Mia:** Ja, wir **spielten** jeden Tag zusammen im Park.
**Tobias:** **Erinnerst** du dich an unseren Lehrer Herrn Schmidt?
**Mia:** Ja, er **war** sehr nett, aber auch streng.
**Tobias:** Was **würdest** du heute gern machen, wenn du frei **hättest**?
**Mia:** Ich **würde** gern ans Meer **fahren**.

**Complete Dialogue:**
**Tobias:** Do you remember when we were children?
**Mia:** Yes, we played together in the park every day.
**Tobias:** Do you remember our teacher Mr. Schmidt?
**Mia:** Yes, he was very nice, but also strict.
**Tobias:** What would you like to do today if you had free time?
**Mia:** I would like to go to the sea.

## Exercise 5: Write About Your Memories and Wishes 📝

**Answer the following questions in complete sentences (3-5 sentences each):**

1. **Was hast du in deiner Kindheit gern gemacht?** (What did you like to do in your childhood?)
2. **Welche schöne Erinnerung hast du an deine Schulzeit?** (What nice memory do you have of your school days?)
3. **Was würdest du heute anders machen, wenn du könntest?** (What would you do differently today if you could?)
4. **Welche Wünsche oder Träume hast du für die Zukunft?** (What wishes or dreams do you have for the future?)

✅ **Sample Answer:**

1. **Was hast du in deiner Kindheit gern gemacht?**
   When I was little, I really liked playing with my brother. We often played soccer in the garden or went bike riding. During the summer holidays we were always at the lake swimming.

2. **Welche schöne Erinnerung hast du an deine Schulzeit?**
   One nice memory from my school days is the trip to the school camp. We hiked in the mountains for a week and sat around the campfire together in the evenings. That was a wonderful time.

3. **Was würdest du heute anders machen, wenn du könntest?**
   If I could, I would start learning German earlier. I would also travel more before I started working. Additionally, I would visit my grandparents more often.

4. **Welche Wünsche oder Träume hast du für die Zukunft?**
   My biggest wish for the future is to live in Germany one day. I want to get my master's degree in Germany and work there. I would also love to travel the whole world and get to know many different cultures.

---

> 💡 **Extra Tip:** The difference between **als**, **wenn**, and **wann**:
> - **als** → past, one-time event: *Als ich 10 war...* (When I was 10...)
> - **wenn** → present or future, or repeated past: *Wenn ich Zeit habe...* (When I have time...), *Wenn wir uns trafen...* (Whenever we met...)
> - **wann** → question about time: *Wann kommst du?* (When are you coming?)
