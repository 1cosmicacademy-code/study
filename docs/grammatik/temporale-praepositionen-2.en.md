## 💡 What Are Temporal Prepositions (Group 2)?

These are further temporal prepositions in German. Each tells us **when** or **within what time frame** something happens:

| Preposition | Meaning | Question |
|:-----------:|:--------|:--------:|
| **bei** | during / at (event or weather) | Wann? |
| **ab** | from / as of (future time) | ab wann? |
| **gegen** | around (approximate time) | gegen wann? / um wie viel Uhr? |
| **zwischen** | between (two times) | zwischen wann und wann? |
| **über** | over / throughout (duration) | wie lange? |

---

## 📊 Comparison Table

| Preposition | Usage | Example | Translation |
|:-----------:|:------|:-------|:------------|
| **bei** | with events and weather | **bei** Regen / **bei** der Arbeit | during rain / at work |
| **ab** | with future time points | **ab** morgen / **ab** 2025 | as of tomorrow / from 2025 |
| **gegen** | with approximate times | **gegen** 18 Uhr | around 6 pm |
| **zwischen** | with a time range | **zwischen** 8 und 9 Uhr | between 8 and 9 |
| **über** | with extended duration | **über** das Wochenende | over the weekend |

---

## 🔍 Detailed Explanation

### bei + Dativ
- **bei** der Arbeit — during work
- **bei** Regen — when it rains
- **bei** Nacht — at night
- **bei** Tisch — at the table (during meals)

### ab + Dativ (often without article)
- **ab** morgen — starting tomorrow
- **ab** nächster Woche — starting next week
- **ab** 18 Uhr — from 6 pm
- **ab** sofort — immediately / from now on

### gegen + Akkusativ
- **gegen** 20 Uhr — around 8 pm
- **gegen** Mitternacht — around midnight
- **gegen** Ende des Jahres — towards the end of the year

### zwischen + Dativ
- **zwischen** 8 und 9 Uhr — between 8 and 9 o'clock
- **zwischen** den Jahren — between Christmas and New Year
- **zwischen** Montag und Mittwoch — between Monday and Wednesday

### über + Akkusativ
- **übers** Wochenende — over the weekend
- **über** den Sommer — throughout the summer
- **über** Nacht — overnight

---

## ✅ Examples in Sentences

- **Bei** Regen bleibe ich zu Hause. — When it rains I stay at home.
- **Bei** der Arbeit höre ich Musik. — During work I listen to music.
- **Ab** morgen habe ich Urlaub. — Starting tomorrow I have vacation.
- **Ab** 18 Uhr ist das Geschäft geschlossen. — From 6 pm the shop is closed.
- **Gegen** 20 Uhr kommen wir an. — We'll arrive around 8 pm.
- **Zwischen** 12 und 13 Uhr habe ich Mittagspause. — Between 12 and 1 I have lunch break.
- **Über** das Wochenende fahre ich nach Berlin. — Over the weekend I'm going to Berlin.
