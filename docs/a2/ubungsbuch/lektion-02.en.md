# 📝 Lektion 2 Exercises: Arbeiten und Lernen (Working and Learning)

> 🎯 **Goal:** Describe professions and personal traits, use adjective endings (Adjektivdeklination)
> 💡 **Tip:** Remember that adjectives take different endings depending on gender (der/die/das) and case (Nominative/Accusative)

## Exercise 3: Adjective Endings (Adjektivdeklination) ✍️

**Complete the sentences with the correct adjective ending:**

1. Das ist ein \_\_\_\_\_\_ (neu) Weg für mich.
2. Die \_\_\_\_\_\_ (freundlich) Kollegin hilft mir immer.
3. Er ist ein \_\_\_\_\_\_ (geduldig) Lehrer.
4. Ich suche einen \_\_\_\_\_\_ (interessant) Job.
5. Das \_\_\_\_\_\_ (klein) Unternehmen sucht neue Mitarbeiter.
6. Sie arbeitet mit \_\_\_\_\_\_ (motiviert) Menschen zusammen.

✅ **Solution:**

| Sentence | Answer | Rule |
|----------|--------|------|
| 1 | ein **neuer** Weg | Masculine Nominative (indefinite article) |
| 2 | Die **freundliche** Kollegin | Feminine Nominative (definite article) |
| 3 | ein **geduldiger** Lehrer | Masculine Nominative (indefinite article) |
| 4 | einen **interessanten** Job | Masculine Accusative (indefinite article) |
| 5 | Das **kleine** Unternehmen | Neuter Nominative (definite article) |
| 6 | mit **motivierten** Menschen | Dative (plural) |

## Exercise 4: Complete the Dialogue (Job Interview) 🎯

**Complete the job interview dialogue between Susanne and Frau Dr. Weber:**

**Frau Dr. Weber:** Good day, Ms. Schmidt. \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Susanne:** Good day, Frau Dr. Weber. Yes, I am looking for a new position.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Susanne:** I studied economics and worked as a manager for five years.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Susanne:** I am organized, a team player, and I learn quickly.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

**Susanne:** Yes, I have a lot of experience in project work. I enjoy that.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** Good, then we'll get back to you next week.

**Susanne:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

✅ **Solution:**

**Frau Dr. Weber:** Good day, Ms. Schmidt. So you're looking for a new challenge?

**Susanne:** Good day, Frau Dr. Weber. Yes, I am looking for a new position.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** What have you done so far?

**Susanne:** I studied economics and worked as a manager for five years.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** What qualities do you bring?

**Susanne:** I am organized, a team player, and I learn quickly.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** Do you like working in a team?

**Susanne:** Yes, I have a lot of experience in project work. I enjoy that.

**Frau Dr. Weber:** Good, then we'll get back to you next week.

**Susanne:** Thank you for the interview. Goodbye!

## Exercise 5: Write About Your Job ✍️

**Write a paragraph (40-60 words) describing your profession or the profession you want. Use the questions as a guide:**

- What is your profession? / What would you like to become?
- What qualities do you need for this profession?
- Do you like working alone or in a team?
- What are you currently learning?

✅ **Sample Solution:**

> By profession I am an architect. I like working creatively and in a team. For my profession you need patience and organization. I am currently learning German in order to work in Germany. My goal is a good job in an international team.
