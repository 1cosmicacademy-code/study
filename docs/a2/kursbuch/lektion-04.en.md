### Rule: Conditional clauses with `wenn`

`wenn` means "if" or "when". It is a conjunction (Konjunktion) that sends the verb to the **end** of the subordinate clause.

| Hauptsatz (Main clause) | Nebensatz mit "wenn" (Subordinate clause with wenn) |
|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|

### Word order with `wenn`

If the sentence starts with `wenn`, the conjugated verb goes to the **end** of the `wenn` clause, and the main clause starts with the verb:

| wenn-Satz am Anfang | Hauptsatz |
