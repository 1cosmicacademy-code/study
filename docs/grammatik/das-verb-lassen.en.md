## 🎯 Lesson Goals

- Understand the different constructions with **lassen**.
- Use **lassen + infinitive**, **lassen + zu + infinitive**, reflexive **lassen**, and **lassen + object + infinitive** correctly.
- Avoid common pitfalls when using *lassen*.

---

## 📚 Core Rule

The verb **lassen** expresses permission, causation, or a request for something to be done. Its pattern changes depending on the complement:
1. **lassen + infinitive** – "let / have someone do something".
2. **lassen + zu + infinitive** – often used with the infinitive plus *zu* to express allowing a completed action.
3. **reflexive lassen** – the subject lets itself undergo an action.
4. **lassen + object + infinitive** – letting an object (person) perform an action.

| Construction | Use | Example (German) | Translation |
|--------------|-----|------------------|------------|
| **lassen + infinitive** | Direct causation / permission | *Ich lasse das Fenster öffnen.* | I let the window open. |
| **lassen + zu + infinitive** | Allow a completed action | *Er lässt das Buch zu Ende lesen.* | He lets the book be read to the end. |
| **reflexive lassen** | Subject allows itself an action | *Sie lässt sich die Haare schneiden.* | She lets her hair be cut. |
| **lassen + object + infinitive** | Permit someone (object) to act | *Der Trainer lässt die Spieler trainieren.* | The coach lets the players train. |

### Typical Errors (Typische Fehler)
- Confusing **lassen** with **machen** – *machen* means “to make/do”, while *lassen* conveys “to let/allow”.
- Adding **zu** to the simple **lassen + infinitive** construction (e.g., *Ich lasse zu gehen* is wrong).
- Placing **lassen** after the infinitive (*Ich öffnen lasse das Fenster*); the auxiliary must precede the infinitive.
- Using **lassen** without a clear agent, leading to ambiguous sentences.

---

## 🎯 Example Sentences

1. *Ich lasse das Auto reparieren.* — "I let the car be repaired."
2. *Lass uns das Meeting verschieben.* — "Let's postpone the meeting."
3. *Er lässt das Buch zu Ende lesen.* — "He lets the book be read to the end."
4. *Sie lässt sich das Haar färben.* — "She lets her hair be dyed."
