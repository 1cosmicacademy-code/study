## 🎯 Exercise Types

- 🖊️ **Fill in the blank** — choose the right word
- 🔗 **Match** — match sentences and meanings
- ✍️ **Write** — create your own sentences
- 💬 **Dialogue** — complete the conversation
- 📝 **Conjugation** — conjugate verbs

> 💡 **Tip:** Try to solve the exercises yourself first, then check the solutions!
